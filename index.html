Picture Connect

100% In-Person Story Game

Pick an image, link it to a theme, and tell your story freely. Listen, react, and enjoy real talk, no pressure.

How to Play

What It Is

A relaxed group game where images inspire short personal stories around a theme. Simple, flexible, and fun for everyone.

Who It’s For

Anyone who wants to connect, practice English, or warm up group energy—teams, students, clubs, and families.

Why It Works

  • Builds natural connection and trust
  • Improves English fluency, confidence, and vocabulary
  • Encourages reflection and creative thinking

What You Need

  • About 40–80 printed images (photos, drawings, icons, or symbols)
  • A simple theme or question to guide conversation

Optional

Sit in a circle or around a table, use sticky notes for new words, and prepare a bowl of random or player-suggested themes.

Images

Images are prompts only—talk about what they make you think or feel, not just what you see.

How to Play (In-Room)

1) Pick a Theme or Question

Examples: Dreams, Change, Travel, Growth, or “When did you feel brave?”.

2) Choose an Image

Pick any image that connects to the theme or question. Take your time.

3) Tell Your Story

Speak for about a minute: describe, reflect, or relate it to your life.

4) One Question or Comment

Another player may ask something or share a short reaction.

5) Rotate Flexibly

The current player chooses the next player or follows clockwise; themes may change anytime.

Guidelines

No strict rules. Skip if you wish. Speak freely, listen kindly.

Example: Dreams

Anna picks a rocket. She dreams of designing spacecraft and the image reminds her to keep reaching higher.

Example: Change

Minh picks a winding road. It reminds him of moving to a new city—turns that led to growth and confidence.
